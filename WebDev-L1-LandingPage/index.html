About Us

More Than Just Coffee

$shetty's Café began with a simple dream: create a space where quality coffee meets genuine community. We source beans from Colombia, Ethiopia, and India's famous coffee regions — Coorg and Chikmagalur in Karnataka — roast in small batches, and serve each cup with intention.

Whether you're here for a morning pick-me-up, an afternoon work session, or a cozy evening with friends — our doors are always open.
